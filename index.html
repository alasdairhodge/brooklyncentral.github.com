Brooklyn simplifies the deployment and runtime management of enterprise-grade applications: single-click deploy to your favourite clouds; keep applications healthy and auto-scaled through your choice of automated policies.

Compose your own applications from a wide range of components and services, and easily write new integrations or policies.

Brooklyn embraces the DevOps philosophy, and uses existing tools like Chef, Salt and jclouds.
